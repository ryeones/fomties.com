figuring out my twenties (fomties) is a series of honest conversations with people in their twenties who have made bold bets on themselves, found their footing, and built something worth talking about.

not just highlight reels. only the real stuff.

ryan sits down with people navigating or just coming out the other side of their twenties, and goes deep on the real stuff: what they're building, what broke them, what they're still figuring out, and what they'd tell their younger self.

---

this started as a personal project. something i wished existed when i was 19, staring at my screen wondering why everyone else seemed to have a plan.

it's part [[stories|podcast]], part [[strategies|newsletter]], part [[spaces|community]]. all of it honest.
