a series of long-form conversations with people in their twenties, or just coming out the other side who made real bets on themselves.

we go deep on the actual stuff: what they were scared of, what broke, what they'd do differently, and what they'd tell their younger self.

no just highlight reels. only their real story.

---

##### what makes it different

most interviews happen after the win. we sit down with people while they're still in it.

that means: more doubt. more pivoting. more honesty about what it actually takes.

the kind of conversation you wish you'd had at 19.
